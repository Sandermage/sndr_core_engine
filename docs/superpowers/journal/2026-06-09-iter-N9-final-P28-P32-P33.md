## Bench methodology key

- **decode_TPOT**: (total - TTFT) / (tokens - 1), the MTP-fair metric — measures pure decode speed independent of TTFT.
- **wall_TPS per-request**: tokens / wall_clock_per_request — what one user sees end-to-end.
- **aggregate_TPS**: total_tokens / wall_time across concurrent requests — what the engine sustains under load.
- **avg_per_req_TPS under conc**: total_tokens / sum(per_req_times) — each user's perceived rate when the engine is loaded (smaller than wall_TPS per-request because of queueing).
- **Stability CV**: cross-run std/mean — measures thermal or scheduler drift between repeats of the same bench.
