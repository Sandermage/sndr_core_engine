## Analysis

- **Monotonic trend**: lower threshold → higher TPS. Threshold=0.1 dominates;
  TPS regression beyond threshold ≥ 0.4-0.5 (no longer beats baseline).
- **Tool quality**: ALL thresholds preserve 7/7 positive cases AND 8/8 if
  including denial. London_think + tokyo_think (Wave 7 boundary cases)
  remain PASS at every setting.
- **Per-model tuning confirmed**: 35B PROD optimum is `threshold=0.3`,
  27B PROD optimum is `threshold=0.1`. Same pattern as Sprint 1 P67
  splits (35B@320K → 48 vs 27B@131K → 32).

## Decision

**Promote 27B PROD YAML: `P82=1` with `threshold=0.1`.**

This is a free +5.23% TPS lift with NO tool-call quality regression.
Same provenance pattern as Sprint 1 P67 promotion (Wave 7 follow-up,
multi-bench validated, monotonic preference within sweep).
